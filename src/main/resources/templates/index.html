How to Play

Playing Lotto is simple and fun. Here's how you can get started:

  • Navigate to the Play Lotto page.
  • Select 6 numbers from 1 to 49.
  • Submit your ticket and optionally provide your email for notifications.
  • Wait for the draw, which happens twice a week on Wednesdays and Saturdays.

Once the draw is conducted, you can check your ticket on the Check Results page to see if you've won!
